After pulling the project, follow the next steps to get started:
1. Open up a terminal and go to [yourCurrentRoute]/backend
2. If you have installed poetry, then execute "poetry shell" on the terminal.
    Otherwise, install poetry with pip
3. If no errors ocurred, execute poetry install and wait
4. execute "python crypto_monkas_backend/app.py", you may have to install the following dependencies with pip:
    - flask_cors
    - pillow (this is the PIL library)
    - pycryptodome (this is the Crypto library)
5. When you're finally able to execute the app.py, open up index.html on the "front" folder.
6. Enjoy your time!
